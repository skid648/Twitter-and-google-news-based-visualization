Political Tweets

Political tweets is an experimental way to observe tweets from current political events. We collect tweets and visualize them in one place.

How does it work

By double clicking a deputy you reveal tweets posted for him. Double click him again to go back to the main graph. Hover a tweet node to read it.

Through time

Last but not least you can select a time stamp from the calendar to restore a previous graph.
Keep in mind that due to pre-alpha stage data collection is not complete. Many timestamps may not build a graph yet.
